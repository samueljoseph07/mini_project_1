Rs 5,000/day

The Volkswagen Virtus offers a spacious cabin, comfortable interior with ample legroom, and modern features like a large infotainment and powered and ventilated front seats. It boasts excellent fuel efficiency, safety features, reliability, and a smooth driving experience.

Pros:
-2023 model update - Added feature, the powertrains are now BS6 Phase 2/RDE compliant

Cons:
