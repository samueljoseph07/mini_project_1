Swift Dzire

Rs 3,000/day

The Dzire is a compact sedan with a unique design. Its USP includes mature ride quality, big boot, interior space for five people, comfort features, and CNG compatibility with its petrol engine.

Pros:
-Spacious, comfortable seats, adequate head-, leg- and shoulder-room.
-Absorbent and flat ride quality whatever the speed one is driving at.
-Refined and peppy motor with less intrusive Auto Gear Shift.
-It has a proportionate design that flows from end to end.
-Strong aftersales network. Excellent resale value.

Cons:
-The Dzire has now begun to look dated.
-Steering feels unresponsive off the straight-ahead position.
-Fit and finish is not class-leading, especially lower down the cabin.
