The all new BMW M340I added to the fleet!

We are thrilled to announce the arrival of the all-new BMW M340i to our fleet! This isn't just any ordinary addition; it's a masterpiece of German engineering designed to exhilarate every drive.

Unleashed Power: The heart of the M340i is a beastly 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine churning out 382 horsepower and 500Nm of torque. That translates to a scorching 0-100 km/h acceleration in a mere 4.4 seconds, leaving you breathlessly exhilarated.

Precision Handling: Carve through corners with confidence thanks to the M Sport suspension. This finely tuned system delivers a sharp and responsive driving experience, making you feel one with the road.

Mesmerizing Interior: Step inside the driver-oriented cockpit and be greeted by a curved display featuring a digital instrument cluster and a large touchscreen infotainment system. Luxurious black Alcantara/Sensatec upholstery with contrasting blue stitching adds a touch of sporty elegance.
