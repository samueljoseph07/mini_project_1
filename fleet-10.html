Audi A4

Rs 9,500/day

The A4, even with the facelift, might still look a tad too subtle compared to the competition, but it's only once you get behind the wheel that you know its true potential. No, it's not a driver's car like the BMW 3, or as charming as the Mercedes-Benz C-Class, but the new A4 is a very fine balance of sporty and elegance.

Pros:
-Performance - Silent/refined petrol motor, ample grunt, provides an enjoyable drive
-Silent ride - No tyre, suspension or external noises, absorbent well-judged suspension
-Comfortable cabin - Nicely contoured seats, generous space (front), good outward view
-Equipment - Equipped with a comprehensive list of modern features like most new-age Audis
-Delightful detailing - Premium feel dash/interiors, solid build, attention to detail

Cons:
-Suited for 4 - Middle occupant gets uncomfy cushioning and bulging air-con console/tunnel
-Simple styling - Doesn't have the bling like other luxury models to attract attention
-Rear headroom - Tall occupants may brush their heads against the roof at the rear
