Terms

1.Driver Requirements:

-Minimum age to rent a car (typically 21 years old).
-Valid driver's license required (an international driver's license may be required in some countries).
-Additional ID verification documents may be needed.

2.Payment and Fees:

-Accepted payment methods for rentals and deposits.
-Explanation of rental rates, including base rate and any additional fees (taxes, one-way drop-off, young driver surcharge, etc.).
-Information on optional insurance coverage (CDW, LIS, PAI) and their associated costs.
-Late return fees and how they are calculated.

3.Vehicle Usage:

-Permitted uses of the rental car (e.g., personal use only, restrictions on off-road driving).
-Limitations on drivers (only authorized drivers listed on the rental agreement can operate the vehicle).
-Fuel policy (who is responsible for refueling and what type of fuel is required).
-Smoking restrictions inside the rental vehicle.

4.Vehicle Return:

-Expected condition of the vehicle upon return (clean, no damage beyond normal wear and tear).
-Procedures for reporting damage or accidents during the rental period.
-Late return policy and associated fees.

5.Liability:

-Renter's financial responsibility for damage to the rental car (depending on the chosen insurance coverage).
-Renter's liability for any traffic violations incurred during the rental period.
