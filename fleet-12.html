BMW M340I

Rs 11,500/day

The M340i impresses with its sharp and exquisite exterior design, excellent performance, and superior road dynamics. Equipped with the latest tech and feature-loaded amenities, its comfortable and luxurious cabin ensures a premium yet sporty driving experience.

Pros:
-The M340i boasts a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that cranks out 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque.
-The car delivers crisp turn-in, responsive steering, and minimal body roll, making it a blast to drive on winding roads.
-The M340i comes standard with rear-wheel drive, but all-wheel drive (xDrive) is available for those who want extra grip in all weather conditions.
-The M340i's cabin is a nice place to spend time, with high-quality materials, comfortable seating, and a plethora of standard features.

Cons:
-The M340i's suspension is on the sporty side, which can make for a somewhat stiff ride on rough roads.
-Some reviewers have found the BMW's iDrive infotainment system to be a bit complex and unintuitive.
-The M340i's trunk is smaller than some of its rivals, which could be a drawback for those who need to carry a lot of cargo.
