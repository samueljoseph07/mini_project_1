FAQ

How do I book a car rental?

You can easily book a car rental on our website. Simply select your pick-up and drop-off locations, choose your desired dates and car type, and follow the prompts to complete your reservation.

Can I book a car by phone?

Yes, you can also book a car rental by calling us at 6305017697. Our friendly customer service representatives are happy to assist you.

Do I need to create an account to book a car?

No, you don't need to create an account to book a car. However, creating an account allows you to save your preferences, view past rentals, and manage upcoming reservations more easily.

What are the age requirements to rent a car?

The minimum age requirement to rent a car is typically 21 years old, although this may vary depending on your location and the car category. A young driver surcharge may apply to renters under the age of 21.

What documents do I need to pick up my rental car?

You will need a valid driver's license, a credit card in your name, and a valid government-issued ID to pick up your rental car.

Are there any additional fees I should be aware of?

There may be additional fees associated with your rental, such as taxes, optional insurances, or one-way drop-off fees. These fees will be clearly outlined during the booking process.
